Chilaquiles Recipe

Description

This classic and iconic mexican breakfast will get you the energy to blast through the day

Ingredients

  • Salsa
  • Eggs
  • Sour Cream
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Totopos/Fried Tortillas
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the egg
  2. Cut the Onion
  3. Put the fried tortillas in the plate
  4. Add the sour cream
  5. Lay the Eggs in the plate
  6. Add the cheese
  7. Add Salsa
  8. Serve hot
